PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: March 15, 2025

INTRODUCTION

Welcome to Mangi. We respect your privacy and are committed to the protection of your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, process, and share your information when you use our mobile application and associated services.

This policy is designed to comply with privacy laws in all jurisdictions where our app is available, with specific provisions for Tanzanian users to ensure compliance with the Electronic and Postal Communications Act (EPOCA), the Cybercrimes Act, and the Personal Data Protection Act.

WHO WE ARE

Mangi is a business management application that provides inventory management, sales tracking, supplier relationship management, customer data management, and other business functions. Our application is designed to run offline and yet provide strong business management capabilities.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Following information we collect:

Personal Information

  • User Information: Name, contact details, login credentials
  • Business Information: Business name, address, business ID, tax ID
  • Employee Information: When adding employees or partners to your account

Transaction and Usage Information

  • Sales Data: Products, quantities, prices, dates, and times
  • Inventory Information: Stock levels, product details, expiry dates
  • Customer Information: Customer names, contact information, purchase history
  • Supplier Information: Supplier details, contact information, supplied products
  • Financial Information: Expenses, revenues, loans, installments
  • Location Data: Business location and login locations
  • Login Time: Login time to monitor business operating hours

Technical Information

  • Device Information: Type of device, operating system, and version
  • App Usage Data: Features used, time, and how you use our app
  • Error Reports: Performance and crash reports
  • Connection Information: Online or offline status

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Providing Our Services: Processing transactions, managing your stock, recording expenses, and other fundamental business activities
  • Improving Our Services: Debugging, creating new features, and improving features
  • Offline Capability: Saving data on your device to allow app usage offline
  • Data Synchronization: Syncing offline data with our servers whenever connectivity is restored
  • Business Analysis: Offering reports and analysis of your business performance
  • Support and Communication: Answering your questions and offering support
  • Security: Keeping your account and data safe from unauthorized use

LEGAL BASIS FOR PROCESSING

We handle your information in accordance with principles that are in line with Tanzania's data protection regulations:

  • Performance of Contract: Processing that is needed in order to be able to offer you our services
  • Legitimate Interests: Where processing is in our legitimate business interests and is not overridden by your rights
  • Consent: Where you have given specific consent for specific types of processing, which can be withdrawn at any time
  • Legal Obligation: Where processing is needed to comply with our legal obligations under Tanzania law
  • Public Interest: Where processing is needed to fulfill a task that is performed in the public interest, as it is required for the Tanzania authorities
  • Vital Interests: Where processing is needed for safeguarding your vital interests or the vital interests of some other person

DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

We can share and disclose your data with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party service vendors who help us in providing and improving our services
  • Business Partners: Whenever we explicitly utilize features to reveal information with suppliers or customers
  • Legal Requirements: Where required by relevant law, regulation, or legal procedure
  • Business Transfers: When in the event of a sale, merger, or acquisition
  • With Your Consent: In other situations explained at collection with your consent

API Access and Data Licensing

If you join our public API program, certain non-personally identifiable business information will be available through our API to third parties on terms of data licensing we decide and according to your configuration settings. All API access and data sharing comply with Tanzania's Electronic and Postal Communications Act (EPOCA) and other legislations related to electronic communications and data sharing.

DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

  • Local Storage: For providing best performance and offline features, your data is locally stored on your device for the most part
  • Synchronization: Where there is internet connectivity, your data can be synchronized with our secure servers to enable backup, recovery, and access on various devices
  • Security Measures: We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to secure protection of your data during storage and transmission
  • Data Protection: Your local and synced data is protected using industry standard encryption and security protocols
  • Data Breach Notification: If your personal data is the subject of a data breach, we will notify you and the appropriate authorities as required by law
  • Data Retention: We will retain your data as long as necessary to run our service and meet legal obligations

Compliance with Regional Requirements

We have appropriate data storage practices to comply with laws of countries that we have a presence in, including maintaining appropriate data localization regulations and maintaining safe cross-border data flows where permissible. For users in Tanzania, we structure our data processing and storage to address the requirements of Tanzania's Electronic and Postal Communications Act (EPOCA) and Cybercrimes Act.

YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES

As one of our service customers, you have considerable rights in respect of your personal data. These rights will vary geographically, but generally comprise:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data we hold
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request erasure of your personal data where it is legally appropriate
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing of your data
  • Data Portability: Request us to port your data to you or a third party where technically feasible
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal data on legitimate interests grounds
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent where we are processing your information on the basis of consent

Additional Rights for Tanzania Users

Tanzanian users have some rights under Tanzania's data protection law, including:

  • All of the above rights
  • The right to complain to the Tanzania Communications Regulatory Authority (TCRA)
  • Other protections under the Personal Data Protection Act

We will respond to you within the time limit specified by applicable law. To exercise these rights, please contact us with the details provided in the Contact Us section.

Remember that some of these rights are restricted where we have significant legitimate interests in processing your data or where there are legal requirements.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our services are not intended for use by individuals below the age of 18 years. As per Tanzania's child protection act, we do not intentionally collect personal information from children under the age of 18. If you are a parent or guardian and you suspect we may have collected information about a child, kindly inform us at once using the contact information below, and we will strive to delete such information and shut down any account created.

INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

Our services could involve the transmission, storage, and processing of your personal data to and within other nations of the world where our servers are located and operations are based.

We take precautions when transferring personal data outside our country, including:

  • Utilizing approved standard contractual clauses from qualified authorities
  • Recipient countries with adequate data protection laws
  • Implementing additional security measures for sensitive information

For all users, including Tanzanian users, we structure our cross-border data transfers in a manner that is respectful of applicable laws and regulations and ensures uninterrupted availability and integrity of our services. Regional requirements for compliance are balanced against making sure that we have access to available, secure, and effective backup systems.

SPECIFIC FEATURES AND PRIVACY IMPLICATIONS

DATA RETENTION AND DELETION

  • Account Information: Active for your account
  • Transaction Data: Stored for fiscal and legal compliance purposes
  • Deletion Requests: Specific data will be retained even in the case of deletion requests due to legal and regulatory purposes

REGIONAL PRIVACY REQUIREMENTS

Global Standards

We pledge to implement the highest global privacy and data protection standards in all nations where our app is made available. Our practices are intended to comply with significant privacy guidelines like:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) guidelines
  • African Union Convention on Cyber Security and Protection of Personal Data

Tanzania-Specific Compliance

For Tanzanian users, we have particular compliance with:

  • Electronic and Postal Communications Act (EPOCA): We meet all the electronic communications and data handling standards.
  • Cybercrimes Act: We have security protocols in place to protect against unauthorized access and cyber attacks.
  • Personal Data Protection Act: We comply with principles for lawful processing of personal data.
  • Tanzania Communications Regulatory Authority (TCRA) Guidelines: We are fully compliant with all relevant TCRA rules and directives.

Local Data Requirements for Tanzania Users

  • Data Localization: Where legally necessary, certain data may be hosted on Tanzanian-based servers.
  • Registration Requirements: We maintain appropriate registrations with appropriate Tanzanian authorities.
  • Phone Number Format: We manage phone numbers as per Tanzania format (numbers starting with 255).
  • National Language Issues: We provide main information in both English and Swahili where required by Tanzanian law.
