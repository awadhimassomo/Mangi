1. Set Up Your Account

Easily create an account and integrate all your business needs in one place—manage suppliers, customers, and transactions effortlessly.

2. Manage Your Daily Business Transactions

In the background, our app creates a connected ecosystem of Mangi apps that ensures your business stays intact. Strengthen relationships with customers and suppliers while also accessing microfinance opportunities to fuel growth.

Frequently Asked Questions

Your Questions, Answered

We've compiled a list of the most common questions we receive to help you understand how Mangi can streamline and grow your business.

Installing the Mangi app is simple! Visit either the Apple App Store or Google Play Store, search for "Mangi Business", and click download. Once installed, sign up to get started.

Mangi is available for both Android and iOS users. Simply visit the Play Store for Android or the App Store for iOS, search for "Mangi Business", and download the app. You can also scan the QR code from our website to download it directly.

Unlike traditional POS systems, Mangi is more than just transactions. It offers a full ecosystem that connects you with suppliers for seamless stock management, provides tools to engage customers, and gives you access to microfinance to grow your business.

Mangi offers a flexible pricing structure. We have a free trial for 7 days, and then our pricing starts at TSH 10,000/month for the basic plan, offering essential tools for small businesses, and goes up to TSH 100,000/month for advanced features including inventory management, customer engagement, and business development support.

To uninstall Mangi, simply go to your phone's settings, navigate to "Apps", select "Mangi Business", and choose the "Uninstall" option. If you face any issues, contact our support team available 24/7.

Security is our top priority. Mangi uses the latest encryption technologies and complies with all major security standards to ensure that your transactions and data are safe and secure. We partner with trusted payment gateways to provide seamless and secure payment processing.
